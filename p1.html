the contemplative mind








































the contemplative mind is an outcome in the search of finding ways to produce an atmospheric description which defines a vague imagination in a very precise way. It is visualised through narratives that helped in construction of spaces and their ambience and gave an account of a not yet existing situation, something which is was conceived only in my thoughts. These descriptions acted as vehicle of empathy, to be able to build spatial themes that give more attention to the experiential factors of architecture. I seek to understand the emotional depth in the perception of the space before the creation of the material space itself.
